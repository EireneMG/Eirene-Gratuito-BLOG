Hi! I am Eirene Gratuito

Hello! I am Eirene Gratuito, a 3rd-year BSIS student.

I'm 20 years old and I live in San Pedro, Laguna. I’m taking up the BSIS course at the Technological University of the Philippines - Manila because I’ve always been curious about technology. I enjoy exploring computers and surfing the internet to learn new things.

Although my course is challenging, I’ve realized that it is very versatile. I’m learning various skills, including programming, networking, and designing, which might lead me to discover a future career path. I also enjoy trying different activities, like playing volleyball and guitar.

These days, I love taking photos with my digicam, something my brother taught me when I was 17 or 18. I enjoy capturing beautiful and interesting things. In my free time, I play video games like Valorant and League of Legends with my siblings.

That’s a brief overview of my life for now, but I’m always excited to learn and experience new things.
